Web Site Critiques

Questions

Business: YOUthDuo / Galactic Spirit  |  jamilin.com

  1. What was your first impression of this site?

    My first impression of this site was its dated appearance compared to other popular websites used today.


  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)

    The flow of information on the main page of makes sense. The links to other areas of the websites are in line at the tope and groups of information are lined up below.


  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?

    The rest of the sites structure does not make sense. Each area of the website is completely different from each other. Also, the header image is massive.


  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?

    The site's multimedia detracts from the site. The images are blurry, and poorly cropped. The embedded videos are everywhere; along the side bar, across the header, and they are very small.


  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

    The load time was very fast, which is very reasonable.


  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

    The text is very small and hard to read. The colors are contrasting so that wasn’t really an issue.


  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

    The site has a navigation bar which organizes the links to other areas of the site quite well.


  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

    The color schemes across the various pages were all over the place. Nearly each page has a different color scheme.


  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?

    When using a smaller window to view the site, the website is next to unusable.


  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

    The site’s embedded videos and the vast amount of links was really unusual.
